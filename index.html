Who we are

Why GDC Chose Abeokuta Over Lagos

Think all data centers belong in Lagos? Think again. GDC’s ABK 1, a 20MW Tier III facility, is rising in Abeokuta — and for good reason.

While Lagos boasts top-tier connectivity and a thriving digital economy, it also faces growing threats from climate risks like flooding.

In October 2024, floods disrupted key infrastructure, reinforcing the need for a more resilient location. That’s where Abeokuta stands tall — secure, connected, and ready.

GDC’s ABK 1: Nigeria’s No. 1 Backup & Disaster Recovery Site

Just 80km from Lagos — around an hour by road or rail — Abeokuta offers a climate-safe zone that drastically lowers environmental risks.

For tech innovators, FinTech firms, and digital enterprises relying on Lagos infrastructure, ABK 1 is the smart fallback: close enough to connect, far enough to protect.
